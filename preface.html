Cattaile Comic Preface

Preface

Despite not having many artistic or game development skills, I decided to contribute to the Cattaile Everything Jam by drawing a comic.

The comic is a non-linear collection of snippets of the Jam featuring ideas, progress, discussions, and some odd scenarios, some from actual conversations in the didacated discord server and some imagined.

The intent was to draw a page a day. I came up with the idea a week into the Jam and had a loaded work schedule so there will to be bits of filler material. However, I have managed to complete 30 pages by the end of October!
